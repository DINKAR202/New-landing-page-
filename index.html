    BMSCE -An Overview

    Sir padampat singhania university(SPSU) is a UGC recognised private university,located in Udaipur Rajasthan.It was established by JK cement limited in 2007. The university has two constituent school under it: school of engineering and school of management, which offer nearly 25 courses at UG ,PG and PHD level.

      Approval and Accreditation

    • Recognized by University Grants Commission ( UGC)
    • Approved by All India Council For Technical Education ( AICTE)
    • Accredited by NAAC with Grade of A++
    • Affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU)
    • Approved by Government of Rajasthan.

    Eligibility criteria

    B.E.

    Passed in 10+2/ Equivalent Examination with English and obtained a Minimum of 45% aggregate marks in Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with Chemistry / BioTechnology / Biology / Computer Science / Electronics as optional subjects.

    Qualifying any one entrance exam is Mandatory. CET/ COMED-K / JEE / AIEEE

    MCA

    Passed B.E/B.Tech in the relevant discipline and obtained 50% aggregate marks (for General Merit Candidates) is eligible for admission.

    45% of marks in case of SC/ST and Cat-I candidates. (Reservation is applicable only for Rajasthan Candidate)

    Taking any one Entrance Examination is mandatory: KMT or PGCET

    M.Tech

    Passed B.E/B.Tech in the relevant discipline and obtained 50% aggregate marks (for General Merit Candidates) is eligible for admission.

    45% of marks in case of SC/ST and Cat-I candidates. (Reservation is applicable only for Rajasthan Candidate)

    A candidate with valid GATE score or PGCET of Rajasthan

    MBA

    Passed B.E/B.Tech in the relevant discipline and obtained 50% aggregate marks (for General Merit Candidates) is eligible for admission.

    45% of marks in case of SC/ST and Cat-I candidates. (Reservation is applicable only for Rajasthan Candidate)

    Taking any one Entrance Examination is mandatory: KMT / CMAT / MAT / PGCET

    Campus life

    SPS College of Engineering (SPS) is located in Udaipur, Rajasthan, India. The campus is spread over 15 acres of land and is well-equipped with modern facilities.

    Hostel: SPS provides on-campus accommodation for students in the form of hostels. The hostels are well-maintained and provide students with a comfortable living environment.

    Sports Facilities: SPS has a range of sports facilities, including a cricket ground, football ground, basketball court, volleyball court, and table tennis room.

    Library: SPS has a well-stocked library with a vast collection of books, journals, and e-resources. The library is open 24/7.

    Clubs and Societies: SPS has a variety of clubs and societies that cater to the interests of students like the Robotics and Internet of Things (IoT) Club, cultural clubs like the Music and Dance Club, and sports clubs like the Cricket and Football Club.

    Festivals and Events: SPS hosts a variety of festivals and events throughout the year, including technical fests like "Exodus" and cultural fests like "Utsav".

    Food and Cafeterias: SPS has a variety of eateries and cafeterias on campus that serve a range of cuisines. The food is hygienic and affordable.
